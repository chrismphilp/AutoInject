    What is AutoInject?

    AutoInject is a tool that allows you to keep your system secure from potential vulnerabilites, by keeping your software protected and up-to-date against the most recently discovered threats.

    How can I use it?

    Use the vulnerabilities page to find what your system is currently insecure against, the logs page to see the updates your system has undergone, and the dashboard to disable particular packages from being updated.

    Also check out the profile page to ensure the system is best suited to you.

    Version

    The system is current operating at version 0.1.

    Dependencies?

    AutoInject dependencies are listed in the setup.py file, at the root of the AutoInject file structure.
